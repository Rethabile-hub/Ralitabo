About TheraPreneur

TheraPreneur launched in 2025 to bring therapeutic coaching to founders, combining business strategy with personal resilience.

Vision

A world where entrepreneurs are calm, clear, and confident in every pivot.

Mission

Deliver evidence-based founder coaching + investor readiness through empathy, metrics, and mental health tools.

Values

Integrity, curiosity, nervous-system health, and sustainable scale.

Founder journey timeline

  1. Q1 2025: Started with 10 founders for therapeutic strategy sessions.
  2. Q2 2025: Built a diagnostics framework for stress + growth alignment.
  3. Q4 2025: Expanded to investor coaching and partner equity mindset training.
